Logic Gates

Logic gates are fundamental components of digital circuits. They perform logical operations on binary inputs and produce binary outputs.

Digital Logic Computer Architecture

Digital logic is the foundation of computer architecture. It deals with the representation and manipulation of data using binary (0s and 1s) values, which are the fundamental building blocks of digital circuits.
